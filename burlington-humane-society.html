Burlington Humane Society

Giving animals a second chance at finding a loving home.

Design Process UX Hypothesis Research Define Prototyping Key Learning

DESIGN PROCESS

UX HYPOTHESIS


THE PROBLEM:

  • •Burlington Humane Society does not provide sufficient information about the animals which makes the adoption process more difficult.
  • •The adoption process is not clear enough for users.
  • •Information on web page is disorganized and overwhelming.
  • •Causing users to not have confidence in their animal adoption of choice.

THE SOLUTION:

We believe that by organizing the information effectively and by clearly outlining the adoption process for our users, this will help us reach our objective of finding a suitable pet for interested adopters.

RESEARCH

We conducted 6 user interviews, and surveyed 67 respondents. From our research, these are the important points that our users wanted to see when they are looking to adopt.

DEFINE


PROBLEM STATEMENT:

We have gathered that users have a challenging time navigating and finding the right pet which causes a low engagement rate for the adoption shelter.

How might we improve the Burlington Humane Society’s website, in helping increase their adoptions rates and provide users with the confidence to find a compatible pet for their family.


USER INSIGHT

We have discovered that a majority of our respondents would like more information on:

  • •Personality
  • •Age
  • •Pet History (Environment)
  • •Neutered/Spayed

Therefore, we believe that if pet adoption websites provide easier access to necessary adoption information, it would make the adoption process easier and giving confidence to pet adopters in finding a compatible match.

PROTOTYPING & TESTING

After understanding the goal and the problem, we were able to look into implementing these features and fully flesh them out into a mid-fi wireframes.

After taking our mid-fi’s into testing we did additional iterations based off our user’s test results and eventually added in our colours transforming it into a hi-fi prototype.

KEY LEARNINGS

  • •Limited with incorporating design solutions as our client wanted to stick to WordPress
  • •Overwhelming amount of animals in shelters that need to be adopted, and by redesigning the website so that it’s user-friendly, it’ll give animals a better chance
  • •Compatibility of animals was very important

NEXT STEPS:

  • •More user testing and iterations
  • •Explore using other website platforms
  • •Look into dating apps and features to compare features to further enhance animal compatibility with adopters
